7 PLACES TO STAY IN Batanes




Batanes : Accommodation


April 18, 2021

Den Calasan


The Batanes group of islands is located at the northernmost tip of Philippine territory. It is known for its scenic waters and terrain and clean environment.






Batanes is an underrated, remote region in the northern Philippines with pastoral emerald hills, rugged landscapes and few tourists.

  1. Midtown Inn Batanes

    2. Located in Basco, 2 km from Valugan Boulder Beach, Midtown Inn Batanes provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking and a terrace. The accommodation features room service, a concierge service and currency exchange for guests.



  2. Batanes Seaside Lodge Annex

    3. Located in Basco, Batanes Seaside Lodge Annex features a terrace. Featuring a shared lounge, this 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom.



  3. Hudhud Homestay

    4. Located in Basco in the Batanes region, Hudhud Homestay provides accommodation with free private parking.



  4. Residencia du Basco

    5. Located in Basco, 3 km from Diura Beach, Residencia du Basco provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking and an outdoor swimming pool. The accommodation offers room service and free shuttle service for guests.



  5. R&E Bed & Breakfast

  6. Featuring a terrace, R&E Bed & Breakfast is set in Ivana. This 1-star bed and breakfast offers free shuttle service and a shared kitchen. Asian cuisine is served at the restaurant.



  7. Nana's Udian Homestay

    8. Boasting a terrace, Nana's Udian Homestay is situated in Basco, within 2.1 km of Valugan Boulder Beach.



  8. Batanes Wakay Homestay

      Batanes Wakay Homestay is located in Basco and offers a terrace. The nearest airport is Basco Airport, 11 km from the homestay.
